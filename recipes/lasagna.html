lasagna

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics:

制作千层面可能很耗时，但结果是值得的。你会在下面的食谱中找到详细的配料清单和一步一步的说明，但让我们来回顾一下基本内容：

Ingredients:

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps:

  1. Make the meat sauce. 做肉酱。
  2. Cook the noodles. 煮面条。
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.制作乳清干酪混合物。
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.按照食谱说明把千层面分层。
  5. Cover with foil and bake.用箔纸盖住，然后烘烤。
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.让千层面在上桌前稍微静置一下。
